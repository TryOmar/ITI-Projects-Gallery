About ITI Projects Gallery

A platform dedicated to showcasing the incredible work of ITI students, fostering innovation, and inspiring the next generation of tech leaders in Egypt.

Our Mission

The ITI Projects Gallery was created to celebrate and showcase the remarkable projects developed by students during their intensive 9-month training program at the Information Technology Institute (ITI).

We believe that every project tells a story of dedication, innovation, and growth. This platform serves as a bridge connecting talented developers with the wider tech community, potential employers, and fellow students seeking inspiration.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can submit projects to the gallery?

Currently, the gallery is open to all ITI students who are part of the 9-month training program. Projects should be developed as part of your coursework or during your time at ITI.

How do I edit my project after submission?

You can edit your project by visiting the Edit Project page. Enter the email address you used during submission to find your projects. Select the project you want to edit and make your changes.

Is my email address visible to others?

No. Your email address is kept private and is only used for project identification when you want to edit your submission. It is never displayed publicly in the gallery to protect your privacy.

What happens if my project is hidden from the gallery?

If your project has been hidden, it may be under review or require some updates. You can still edit your project and update the information. Once changes are made, an administrator will review and may make it visible again.

Can I submit multiple projects?

Yes! You can submit as many projects as you like. Each project should have its own submission with unique details. All projects submitted with the same email address can be managed from the Edit page.
